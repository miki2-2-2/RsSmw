get_cabw() → RsSmw.enums.SystConfBbBandwidth[source]
# SCPI: SCONfiguration:CABW
value: enums.SystConfBbBandwidth = driver.sconfiguration.get_cabw()

Queries the resulting carrier aggregation bandwidth, i.e. the signal bandwidth at the outputs of the stream mapper. The value is calculated automatically and depends on the selected MIMO configuration, the installed options and the selected baseband bandwidth (method RsSmw.Sconfiguration.bbBw) . See ‘Welcome to the Fading Simulator’.

return

# SCPI: SCONfiguration:MODE
value: enums.SystConfMode = driver.sconfiguration.get_mode()

Switches between the operating modes.

return

mode: ADVanced| STANdard| REGenerator | GNSS | ESEQuencer ADVanced|STANdard Switches between the and . REGenerator Enables the R&S SMW to work as a radar echo generator. The fading simulator is disabled. See ‘Welcome to the Option’. GNSS Enables the R&S SMW to work as in advanced GNSS mode. The fading simulator is disabled. See ‘Welcome to the GNSS Satellite Navigation Options’. ESEQuencer Enables the R&S SMW to work in an advanced extended sequencer mode. The fading simulator, the AWGN, the BB input and all baseband digital standards are disabled. See ‘Welcome to the Extended Sequencer’.
